Ghoulbane Quest — Quest Flow
----------------------------------------

1. Speak with Sir Ariam Arcanum in Qeynos Hills.
   - He explains the history of Ghoulbane and the need to reforge it.

2. Travel to Everfrost Peaks and locate Miragul’s cave.
   - Speak with the Spirit of Miragul for lore and hints.

3. Defeat the Lich of Miragul.
   - Loot Miragul’s Fragment (ID 720002).

4. Acquire the remaining components:
   - Ghoulbane Token (ID 720001)
   - Purified Hilt (ID 720003)
   - Blessed Oil (ID 720004)

5. Return all four items to Sir Ariam Arcanum.
   - He reforges Ghoulbane and rewards:
     Ghoulbane (Pre-Nerf) (ID 720005)

This quest flow restores the classic Ghoulbane reforging process using
historically accurate NPCs, items, and dialogue.
